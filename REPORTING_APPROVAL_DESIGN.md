# Reporting Approval Design

## Unified Reporting Views by Clinic
- Define clinic-wide reporting metrics, including patient demographics, treatment outcomes, and revenue generation.
- Create visual dashboards that allow for quick insights into clinic performance.

## Salary Details
- Outline salary components for each role, including base pay, bonuses, and allowances.
- Provide detailed reporting on salary expenditures per clinic and per role.

## Summaries
- Develop summary reports that include key performance indicators across clinics.
- Summaries should highlight strengths, weaknesses, and areas for improvement.

## Approval Workflow Design
- Describe the workflow for approving reports, including roles and responsibilities.
- Implement stages of review, feedback channels, and final approval processes.
