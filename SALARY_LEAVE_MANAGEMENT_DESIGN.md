# Salary Leave Management Design

## Features

### Salary Calculation
- Calculate monthly salary based on hours worked.
- Include overtime calculations if applicable.
- Allow for deductions (taxes, insurance, etc.) to arrive at net pay.

### Leave Management
- Track various types of leave (sick, annual, etc.).
- Allow employees to apply for leave via the system.
- Managers can approve or deny leave requests.

### Special Leave Calculation
- Define conditions for special leave (maternity, paternity, etc.).
- Automatic calculation of special leave credits.

### Time Tracking
- Track attendance using a clock-in/clock-out system.
- Generate reports on hours worked, including late arrivals and early departures.
- Integrate with salary calculations for accurate payroll processing.
